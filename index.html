About Our School

Deoria Senior Secondary School, Deoria (Formerly: Deoria Girls College, Deoria), administered by Dr. Girish Naraian Gupta and looked after by Deoria Girls College Trust, is a premier English medium school of Eastern Uttar Pradesh. The school is the fulfilment of the long-cherished dream of Dr. G. N. Gupta and the response of the people of Deoria here to render quality education studying. This school was established with 35 girl’s students of class - VII in 2003. The school got NOC from the State Government on 30th July 2004 and affiliation to the CBSE Board AISCE in 2004.

Some important facts

Deoria Senior Secondary School is a reputed educational institution located in the city of Deoria, Uttar Pradesh. Here are some important facts about the school: Established in 2003, Deoria Senior Secondary School has a rich history of providing quality education to students in the region. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), which is a nationally recognized board of education in India. The school offers classes from kindergarten to grade 12 and has a student strength of over 1000. The school has a team of dedicated and qualified teachers who are committed to providing students with the best possible education. The school has a well-equipped library, computer lab, and science lab, which provide students with access to the latest resources and technologies. The school has a strong focus on extracurricular activities and encourages students to participate in various sports, music, dance, and art programs. Deoria Senior Secondary School has a proven track record of academic excellence, with many students achieving excellent grades in various national-level examinations. The school has a strong alumni network, with many former students occupying prominent positions in various fields such as business, government, and academia. The school is committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment for students to learn and grow and has various measures in place to ensure the safety and security of its students. Deoria Senior Secondary School is a proud member of the local community and actively participates in various community service initiatives and programs.

Make Your Child Standout

As parents, we all want our children to succeed in life and make a mark for themselves. But in today's fast-paced and competitive world, simply getting good grades is not enough. To truly stand out and succeed, our children need to develop a range of skills and qualities that go beyond academic excellence. Here are some tips on how you can help your child stand out: Encourage Creativity: Creativity is a key skill that can set your child apart from others. Encourage your child to explore their creative side by engaging in activities such as painting, writing, music, or dance. These activities can help develop their creativity, imagination, and problem-solving skills. Build Communication Skills: Effective communication is essential for success in all walks of life. Encourage your child to participate in debates, public speaking events, or drama, which can help build their communication skills and confidence. Foster Leadership Skills: Leadership skills are essential for success in all fields. Encourage your child to take up leadership roles in school or community activities, which can help them develop skills such as decision-making, teamwork, and problem-solving. Develop Emotional Intelligence: Emotional intelligence refers to the ability to understand and manage one's own emotions and those of others. Encourage your child to develop emotional intelligence by practicing empathy, active listening, and problem-solving. Focus on Well-being: A healthy body and mind are essential for success in all areas of life. Encourage your child to engage in physical activities such as sports or yoga, which can help improve their physical and mental well-being. By following these tips, you can help your child develop the skills and qualities that can set them apart from others and help them succeed in life. At Deoria Senior Secondary School, we are committed to providing our students with a holistic education that focuses on developing their overall personality and helping them become successful and responsible members of society.
